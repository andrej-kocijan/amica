Getting your GU / Chalmers Card

If you are a GU student, you should visit one of the service points to get your card, you will be guided through the proccess. The same goes for Chalmers, but you should visit the service center at the Johanneberg campus.

Entering class rooms on campuses

The combination of the card you have recieved and the PIN you have set, will allow you to enter locked class rooms and give you campus access out of office hours. The proccess is straight forward, scan you card, input your PIN and the door should unlock for you
Note: if you are a GU student, studying at Chalmers, you might have problems unlocking doors at Johanneberg, that is easily solvable by visiting the service center at Chalmers and merging your GU and Chalmers cards
