Transport

How to find your way?

install Vässtraffik app on the Play/Apple store, you can put in in english. From there you can buy, store, view your tickets, find your route & see tram/bus/ferries traffic.

Where can i go with a Zone A ticket ?

This is enough for living in gothenburg, and moving from the city center up to the outside, take ferries from Gothenburg to the nearest archipelago and to the outside of the city (sports fields, the sea...).

How to get the Student Discount ?

You need to have your Mecenat card. Without a valid Mecenat card, your student ticket will be invalid and you will be fined.

So at your arrival, you will need to buy a standard adult ticket until you receive your mecenat card. More info on the mecenat card on their website : *link* or on the section University.
