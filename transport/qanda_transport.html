Transport

▶ Will they check for my ticket on public transport?
You do not have to validate your monthly tickets each ride, but controls are performed at random (and they are not that rare). If you are using a student ticket, they may ask you for your mecenat card as well.
▶ Do I require a mecenat card to purchase a students ticket?
A mecenat card is not required for purchasing, but your ticket is invalid without a mecenat card.
▶ Tram or bus?
Plane
▶ Is the public transport fast?
Sometimes
▶ What is the penalty for being caught with no ticket?
750SEK if you are under 18 years of age, 1500SEK else, so buy your ticket!
